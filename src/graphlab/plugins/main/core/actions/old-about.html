GraphLab v0.9.1

Graphlab is a modular,pluggable ,easy-to-use and easy-to-develop platform based on scientific mathematical descriptions in graph theory;  capable of working with graphs and graph algorithms.Graphlab is developed at Mathematical Science dept. of Sharif Unviersity of Tech.It is written under Java programming language and is liscensed under the  GPL.
